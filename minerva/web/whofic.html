Welcome to the WHO FIC Implementation Database

The WHO FIC Implementation database is a web-based platform for collection and dissemination of general information on the implementation of the WHO Family of International Classifications (WHO-FIC), including the International Classification of Diseases (ICD, the International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health (ICF), the International Classification of Traditional Medicine (ICTM), and others in WHO Member States.

WHO-FIC implementation refers to the use and application of the WHO classifications, and their related instruments, in the following areas: morbidity, mortality, health and disability statistics, legislation & policy, health and social service provision, and research. The database also includes generic information relevant to WHO-FIC implementation (available language versions, training material, IT infrastructure, etc.)

The information in the database shall inform, guide and facilitate the exchange of information on where and how WHO-FIC Classifications are used on a general level.

Under WHO coordination, the database is populated and updated regularly by identified focal point from the WHO-FIC Collaborating Centers as well as key informants from relevant countries.
